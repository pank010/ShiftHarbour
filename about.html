ShiftHarbour was born in Fredericton with one idea: make short-term hiring as simple as a text message — for businesses and workers alike.

Our story

Built for the gaps that other platforms ignore

Every weekend in Fredericton, hotels scramble for banquet servers, restaurants need last-minute kitchen help, and event venues can't fill their floor staff fast enough. Meanwhile, hundreds of locals want flexible work — but have no easy way to find it.

ShiftHarbour started as a simple question: why does it take days to fill a 6-hour shift? The answer was that no platform was built specifically for Atlantic Canada's short-term, event-driven economy.

We're changing that — starting right here in Fredericton.

What we stand for

Our values

Every feature we build is guided by these three principles.

🤝
Direct connections only
No middlemen, no recruiters, no agency fees. Employers and workers connect directly — keeping more money in everyone's pocket.
⚡
Speed matters
A shift that starts tomorrow can't wait three days for a response. We built ShiftHarbour so you can post a job in 2 minutes and get applicants within hours.
🌊
Built for Atlantic Canada
We're not a Toronto startup trying to expand east. We're from New Brunswick. We understand the local economy, the seasonality, and the community.
🔍
Transparency first
Every job shows the real pay rate. Every employer gets real applicant contact info. No hidden fees, no bait-and-switch.
🏘️
Community-driven
ShiftHarbour's growth is shaped by the NB businesses and workers who use it. We survey our users, listen to feedback, and build what actually helps.
🤖
AI that actually helps
Our AI matching extracts worker skills from resumes and ranks them against job requirements — so the right people find the right jobs faster.
