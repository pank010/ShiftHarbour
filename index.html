How it works

Simple for both sides

No recruiters, no agencies — a direct connection between NB businesses and workers.

🏢For Employers
01

Create a free account

Sign up as an employer in under a minute. Verified employers can post unlimited jobs.

02

Post your job

Fill in the role, pay, date and location. Goes live instantly for local workers to see.

03

Receive applications

Workers apply directly. You get their name, phone and email — contact them to confirm.

04

Manage from dashboard

View all your postings, see applicants, and delete posts once filled.

🙋For Workers
01

Create a worker account

Sign up in seconds. Your profile lets employers know who's applying.

02

Browse available jobs

Filter by location, pay and type. See all live NB short-term jobs in one place.

03

Apply in one tap

Submit your details in 60 seconds. Track your applications from your dashboard.

04

Show up & get paid

Employer contacts you to confirm. Complete the shift and build your local reputation.
