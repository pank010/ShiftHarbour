How it works

From posting to placed,
in three steps.

No recruiters. No agencies. A direct connection between businesses and workers who are ready to go.

01

Post your job

Businesses list the role, pay rate, date, and location. Takes less than two minutes and reaches local workers immediately.

02

Build your profile

Workers create a profile with their skills, availability, and experience. Get discovered by businesses looking for exactly your background.

03

Get connected

ShiftHarbour matches the right worker to the right opportunity. Show up, do great work, and build a trusted local reputation.
