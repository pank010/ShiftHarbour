The plan

Our expansion timeline

Four deliberate phases — each one building on the last. We're not rushing growth; we're building something that lasts.

Phase 1 — Now January 2026 – June 2026
Fredericton Pilot Launch
Build the platform, prove the concept, gather real user feedback from Fredericton's hospitality, event, and retail sectors.
📍 1 city 🎯 100 employers 👥 500 workers 💰 Free to all

🌊
Phase 2 — 6 months July 2026 – December 2026
Atlantic Canada Expansion
Roll out to Moncton, Saint John, Halifax, Charlottetown and St. John's. Target the hospitality, catering and event industries across all four Atlantic provinces.
📍 5+ cities 🏝️ 4 provinces 🎯 500 employers 👥 5,000 workers 💼 Gig economy focus
Phase 3 — 2027 Q1 2027 – Q3 2027
Central Canada — Ontario & Québec
Expand to Toronto, Ottawa, Montréal and surrounding regions. Introduce premium employer tiers. Target corporate event staffing and large hospitality chains.
📍 10+ cities 🎯 2,000 employers 👥 25,000 workers 💰 Revenue model active

Phase 4 — 2028 Q1 2028 onwards
National Coverage — Coast to Coast
Full Canadian presence — Vancouver, Calgary, Edmonton, Winnipeg and beyond. Become Canada's #1 platform for short-term and gig-economy work.
🍁 National platform 📍 25+ cities 🎯 10,000 employers 👥 100,000+ workers
