1. Who We Are

ShiftHarbour is a local staffing platform that connects businesses in New Brunswick, Canada with reliable local workers for short-term and event-based employment opportunities.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide the platform: Matching employers with suitable workers, displaying job listings, and processing applications
  • Account management: Creating and managing your ShiftHarbour account, authenticating your identity
  • Communications: Notifying employers when workers apply to their jobs, sending confirmation emails, and responding to your inquiries
  • Platform improvement: Understanding how the platform is used so we can improve it during our pilot phase
  • Research: Analysing aggregated, anonymised data to understand the New Brunswick staffing market
  • Legal compliance: Complying with applicable Canadian laws and regulations

We will never use your information for targeted advertising, sell it to data brokers, or use it for any purpose unrelated to operating ShiftHarbour.

4. How We Share Your Information

ShiftHarbour does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

Between Employers and Workers

When a worker applies for a job, their application details (name, phone, email, experience, and availability) are shared with the employer who posted that job. This is the core function of our platform. By submitting an application, you consent to this sharing.

Job posting details (including the employer's contact email) are visible to registered workers on the platform.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. Supabase stores data in data centres located in the United States. By using ShiftHarbour, you consent to your data being transferred to and stored in the United States.

We implement the following security measures:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit)
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Database access is restricted using Row Level Security policies
  • API keys are restricted to minimum necessary permissions
  • Regular security reviews during our pilot phase

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Job postings are retained until deleted by the employer or removed by ShiftHarbour. Applications are retained for 12 months after submission. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Cookies & Tracking

ShiftHarbour uses minimal cookies and tracking technologies:

  • Authentication cookies: Used to keep you signed in to your account. These are essential for the platform to function.
  • Session storage: Used temporarily to remember your role selection and pending actions during a browsing session. This data is cleared when you close your browser.

We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking pixels, or third-party analytics tools that track you across websites. We do not participate in any ad networks.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may prevent you from signing in to your account.

7. Your Rights

As a resident of New Brunswick (and Canada), you have rights under Canadian privacy law, including the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA):

  • Right to access: You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to correction: You can request that we correct inaccurate information about you
  • Right to deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Right to withdraw consent: You can withdraw your consent to our processing of your information at any time by deleting your account
  • Right to complain: You have the right to file a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada if you believe your privacy rights have been violated

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected]. We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

Deleting Your Account

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing [email protected] with the subject line "Account Deletion Request." We will process your request within 7 business days.

8. Children's Privacy

ShiftHarbour is intended for users who are 16 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at [email protected] and we will delete that information.

Workers must be of legal working age in New Brunswick (minimum 14 years with parental consent for certain jobs, 16 years for most employment) to use the platform. By creating a worker account, you confirm you meet the minimum age requirement for the type of work you are seeking.
